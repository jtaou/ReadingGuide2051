Chapter 3: The First Law of Thermodynamics:


Introduction:


-Is energy conserved in thermonynamic process? Yes.


3.1 Thermodynamic Systems:


-What is thermodynamic system? Anything whose thermodynamic properties are of interest.

-What is boundary? Imaginary wall that separates the system with the environment.

-What is closed system? sytem completely seperated from its environment.

-What is open system? system which can exchange energy and/or matter with surroundings.

-What dictates the thermodynamic equilibrium of a system? heat transfer between system and its envrionment or between the different parts of the system.

-What is equation of state? Relationship between basic properties of the system.

-What is extensive variables? Give examples.Extensive: quantity associated with an amount of matter. eg. volume, number of moles.

-What is intensive variables? Give examples. Intensive: quantity not associated with amount of matter. eg. pressure, temperature.

3.3 First Law of Thermodynamics:


-What is first law of thermodynamics? The change in internal energy for any transition between two equilibrium state = heat exchanged - work

-When is work done -ve (+ve)?When work is done on the system (by the system).

3.4 Thermodynamic Processes:


-What are the thermodynamical variables for an ideal gas? pressure, volume, temperature and number of molecules.

-What is isothermal process? change in state of the system at a constant temperature.

-What is isobaric and isochoric processes? Isobaric: pressure constant. Isochoric: Volume constant.

-What is adiabatic process?Absence of heat exchange.

-Why does the adiabatic expansion leads to drop in temperature? System does work and energy goes down and hence the decrease in temperature.

-Why does the adiabatic compression leads to increase in temperature? Work is done on the system and energy increases.

-What is cyclic process?if the state of the system at the end is same as at the begining.

-What remain unchanged during the cyclic process? Temperature, volume and internal energy.

-What will be the relation between the heat exchanged and work done in cyclic process? Equal.
